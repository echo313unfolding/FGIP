# Investment Policy Statement

**Beneficiary:** Mom
**Prepared:** 2026-03-03
**Review:** Monthly (1st of each month)

---

## Objectives

- **Time Horizon:** 10 years
- **Primary Goal:** Preserve purchasing power; modest growth
- **Risk Tolerance:** Moderate

---

## Constraints

| Constraint | Value |
|------------|-------|
| Minimum liquidity | 18 months |
| Max expense ratio | 20 bps (0.20%) |
| Single stocks | Prohibited |
| Max single position | 30% |
| Tax sensitivity | Yes |

---

## Rebalance Rules

1. **Calendar:** Review allocation every 3 months
2. **Drift:** Rebalance if any bucket drifts >5% from target
3. **Regime:** Adjust per directive triggers on regime change

---

## Circuit Breakers

**No Impulse Rule:** Any allocation change >5% requires 72-hour cooling period before execution.

**Rationale:** Prevents emotion-driven decisions. Sleep on it.

---

## Core/Satellite Structure

| Component | Allocation | Purpose |
|-----------|------------|---------|
| Core | 85% | Safety floor + income ladder + inflation hedge |
| Satellite | 15% | Growth allocation (VTI) |

Core is non-negotiable. Satellite can flex based on regime.

---

## Governance

Decision makers: Mom, Son
Unanimous required: No

---

## What Would Trigger Review

- Regime change (NORMAL -> STRESS/CRISIS)
- FCI exceeds CPI by >2% for 3 months
- Major life change (health, housing, income)
- Annual scheduled review

---

*This IPS governs the family fund. Review monthly. Update annually or on major life changes.*
